Welcome to Genshin Impact

About Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is an open-world action role-playing game developed and published by miHoYo. It features a fantasy open-world environment and action-based battle system using elemental magic and character-switching, and uses gacha game monetization for players to obtain new characters, weapons, and other resources.

Characters in Genshin Impact

There are several unique characters in Genshin Impact, each with their own special abilities and elements. Players can build a party of up to four characters to complete quests and explore the world.

Weapons in Genshin Impact

In Genshin Impact, weapons are equipment that assist the player in combat to deal more damage1. There are five different types of weapons: swords, claymores, bows, polearms, and catalysts12. Each character is only able to wield one weapon type

Here is a brief overview of each weapon type:

  1. Swords: These are melee-range weapons that can easily inflict knockback on foes. They are lightweight and quick, but do not hit as hard as a Claymore
  2. Claymores: These are melee-range weapons that hit slower than Swords and Polearms, but deal much more damage per swing. They are considered Blunt Attacks and deal increased damage to Geo objects, such as monster shields
  3. Polearms: These have fast attacks, long forward reach, and deal moderate damage per hit.
  4. Catalysts: Due to their magical nature, all Normal and Charged Attacks performed with a Catalyst are considered elemental damage. The element of the damage depends on the wielder natural element/vision
  5. Bows: Bows boast long-ranged attacks, with Charged Attacks that are capable of hitting monsters as far as the eye can see with the proper aim. A fully-Charged Attack deals elemental damage
